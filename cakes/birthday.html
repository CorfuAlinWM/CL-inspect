Birthday Cakes


Every birthday deserves a celebration and every celebration deserves an ice cream cake. Carvel has a variety of cakes from our famous Fudgie the Whale® to a 3D Racecar Cake, Birthday Sprinkle Cake, and even a Crazy Silly Emoji Cake. And since each cake is handmade in-shoppe, they can be customized or personalized to match any birthday style. Order ahead online or find a Carvel near you to pick up the perfect Carvel ice cream birthday cake.
