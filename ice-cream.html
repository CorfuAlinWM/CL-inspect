Ice Cream


If you’re looking for an ice cream place near you, look no further than Carvel. In the years since Tom Carvel first set out to bring ice cream and a happy escape to his neighbors in 1929, the Carvel name has become synonymous with smiles. Our shoppes aren’t just a place for ice cream, but a local hub for friends and family to relax and enjoy each other’s company over a cone or cup. It’s also the perfect place to pick up a quart or pint of our newest and classic favorite flavors of scooped and soft serve ice cream to share. Find your nearest Carvel today for a well-deserved treat.
