What is a Specialty Shake?


Our limited-run Specialty Shakes are the Carvel version of the irresistible, crazy creations affectionately known as “Freakshakes.” Our Specialty Shakes take old-school milkshakes to the next level, complete with the Carvel ice cream you love and other outrageous toppings. They’re a party in a cup that falls somewhere between drink and dessert, which is why you need a spoon AND a straw to enjoy one. Try our latest featured specialty shake at a Carvel shoppe near you.
